About LFB

Our Story

I have seen organizations that help with food, shelter, and disease. That is good. But it is not enough.


LFB - Lift Forward Better came to engulf everything.


We lift people who have food but have problems at home. We move forward people who have everything but lack business skills. We make things better for children who need school fees, books, and pens.


Our Mission: No person in Lilongwe should struggle alone. Whether it is food, education, family, or business — LFB is here.
