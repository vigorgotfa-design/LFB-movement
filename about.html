Our Story

I have seen organizations in Malawi that help with food, shelter, and disease. That is good. But it is not enough.


LFB - Lift Forward Better was born right here in Malawi to engulf everything.


We lift families in Lilongwe, Blantyre, and Mzuzu who have food but are breaking at home. We move forward youth in Malawi who have talent but lack business skills and capital. We make things better for children in Malawian schools who need school fees, books, and pens to stay in class.


Our Mission for Malawi: No person in Malawi should struggle alone. Whether it is food, education, family, or business — LFB is here for our nation first.


Our Vision for Africa: What we build in Malawi today, we want to see spread across Africa tomorrow. A continent where every community lifts each other forward.
