About LFB

Lift Forward Better is a movement started in Lilongwe, Malawi.

Lift

We meet urgent needs with food and basic support for families.

Forward

We teach practical skills: gardening, solar farming, and business basics.

Better

We help people start small businesses to provide for themselves long-term.

What We Do

  • Food & Basic Needs: Monthly feeding and support for families facing hardship.
  • Skills Training: Backyard gardening, cooking, and business training.
  • Business Building: Micro-support to help people start selling, farming, or trading.
